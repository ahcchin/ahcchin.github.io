About Me

Raised in beautiful British Columbia, I discovered my passion for technology when I coded my first webpage on a cartoon character in elementary school. Educated on the east coast, I studied Electrical Engineering at University of Waterloo, and got a Masters in ECE at Cornell University. I have worked at companies such as Square, Apple and NVIDIA in Silicon Valley.

In my spare time, I enjoy practicing Iyengar yoga, foodspotting, and instagramming.
