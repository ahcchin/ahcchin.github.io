Square Wallet 3.0 Redesign

As the Program Manager for Square Wallet, I worked heavily with designers and engineers on executing the redesign of the app.

I particularly focused on the overall information architecture, content inventory and user flows.

The result is a product that is more intuitive, more scalable and delivers a delightful user experience.

Square Gift Card

Square Gift Card allows you to instantly send a gift to anyone, anytime. As the PM of the product, I've led the development of the entire online-to-offline gifting experience. The complex user flow begins with:

  • 1) sending a gift card with Square Wallet available on iOS/Android
  • 2) receiving an email & claiming the gift onto your device
  • 3) redeeming the gift at a Square merchant in person

The project was a massive cross-functional effort across multiple departments. The product was successfully launched for the Christmas holiday season.
